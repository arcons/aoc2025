# Day 15: Beverage Bandits

## Goal
Simulate battle between Goblins and Elves.

## Rules
- Units: HP 200, AP 3.
- Turns: Reading order.
- Move: Towards nearest in-range square. Tie-break reading order.
- Attack: Adjacent enemy with lowest HP. Tie-break reading order.
- End: No targets.

## Part 1
Outcome = full rounds * total HP.

## Part 2
Find min Elf AP such that Elves win with NO deaths.
