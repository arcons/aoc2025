# Specification Day 9

## Input
List of coordinates 'x,y'.

## Part 1
Max area of rectangle with two red tiles as corners.

## Part 2
Max area of rectangle with two red tiles as corners, contained in polygon defined by red tiles sequence.
Constraints:
- Polygon edges are axis-aligned.
- Polygon is simple (implied).

## Examples

### Example 1
Input:
7,1
11,1
11,7
9,7
9,5
2,5
2,3
7,3

Part 1 Output: 50
Part 2 Output: 24
